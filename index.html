About me

Roald Nefs is a consultant at Warpnet B.V. with over five years of DevOps experience at various companies like DUO and KPN. He has made several contributions to open source, to tools such as SaltStack, Prometheus, rfcat and many more. Roald enjoys tinkering with electronics, hardware hacking, and software development in general.

You can get in touch with me by email at info@roaldnefs.com (PGP), Twitter (@roaldnefs), and LinkedIn.
