About me

Roald Nefs is an IT consultant at Warpnet B.V. with over five years of Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) and DevOps experience at various companies like DUO and KPN. He has made several contributions to open source, to tools such as SaltStack, Prometheus, rfcat and many more. Roald enjoys tinkering with electronics, hardware hacking, software development and dogsport.

You can get in touch with me by email at info@roaldnefs.com (PGP), Twitter (@roaldnefs), and LinkedIn.
